MOTION GRAPHIC PROJECT

MY ROLE

Graphic Designer, Animator

DELIVERABLES

Animation

PROJECT DURATION

Feb 12th 2022 - Feb 26th 2022 (2 weeks)

BRIEF

I decided to focus on mental health problems, specifically on the negative impacts of toxic positivity, which I have been struggling with for a long time. Unlike other mental health issues that are well-known, toxic positivity is often ignored by individuals since it tends to be well-intentioned. The problem is, life is not always positive. It is almost always complicated, full of ups and downs, and I could choose to face it positively or negatively. I should be able to cry in the face of loss, as long as this action can ease my emotions. I hope this motion graphic could encourage these “non-positive” individuals to accept their negative emotions, and bravely relieve them in their preferred ways.

PROJECT GOAL

I hope this motion graphic could encourage these “non-positive” individuals to accept their negative emotions, and bravely relieve them in their preferred ways. I also hope that all the vloggers or internet celebrities who encourage people to “stay positive in order to be healthy” will understand the impacts of toxic positivity after viewing my graphics.

THE PROBLEM

Users spend lots of time deciding what food they should get, the food delivery status is not shown in detail, and customers are likely to be irritated since they do not know why it takes so long.

MY CONTRIBUTION

I am the sole designer that works on this project; therefore, I fulfilled all of the client requests myself.

TARGET USER

- People who are currently suffering from toxic positivity, tell them they are not alone and it is normal to feel unhappy

- Vloggers or internet celebrities who persuade people to be happy no matter what, tell them to stop toxic positivity.
